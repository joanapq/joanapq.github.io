The New Moon Murder

Home

The case

On his first case with the Investigation Institute, they sent Deductive Logico to the darkest woods he's ever seen. From the shadows, he watched a witches' sabbath, thinking he was totally hidden. So he was shocked when three witches stopped the ceremony, walked over to him, and asked him to solve the murder of their sister witch...

Rules of logic

  • Each suspect was in a separate location and had access to a different weapon at the time of the murder.
  • The murderer is lying. The others are telling the truth.
